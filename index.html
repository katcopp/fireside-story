It was a dark and stormy night... when the alien broke into the bakery.

He was thankful that some of the inhabitants of this planet were into tasty desserts unlike his host family who kept feeding him seaweed.

He went for the eclairs first and midway through gobbling the massive stack of chocolate glazed goodness, he heard the low growl of the beast from the darkness of the kitchen.

He stuffed one more eclair into his mouth as he ran to the kitchen.
